Q2 How to handle the inability to move the screen in Rolox？

A: During automatic quick clicks, the system cannot recognize manual clicks, and timely recognition can also lead to system errors. It is recommended to stop the task before performing screen operations。

Q3 Overall reminder: barrier-free service needs to be enabled？

A: Google has strict permissions requirements, so every time you restart an application, you need to restart it. However, you can solve this problem by turning on automatic startup permissions。

Q4 Automatic clicker exits abnormally and cannot be clicked for a long time？
